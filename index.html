Our History

We opened our doors in 2019 right here in Clarksville, TN with one goal—do great cuts and make people feel at home. The Kingdome is a proud Latino and veteran-owned business that started as a simple idea: create a space where anyone can sit down, get a fresh fade, and have a real conversation. No frills, just quality work and good vibes. Over the years, it’s become more than a shop, it’s a place where friendships are made, stories are shared, and every client is treated like family.

We are barbers who love our craft, providing top notch service that helps the community Stay Sharp.
